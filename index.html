Welcome

At InnerSpark Studio, we believe every child carries a tiny spark of creativity, curiosity, and wonder.

Our mission is to ignite these sparks through education, creativity, and emotional growth — shaping confident and joyful learners.

Through storytelling, playful exploration, and meaningful learning experiences, we light up each child’s path with connection, imagination, and heart.

We believe that nurturing a child's mind and heart can light up their whole future.

Join us on this magical journey of learning and dreaming together!

Our Programs

Guidebooks for Educators

Evidence-based resources grounded in the science of reading and neuroscience to empower teachers.

Magical Stories for Children

Engaging stories designed to ignite creativity while supporting early literacy development.

Interactive Flashcards & Activities

Phonics, vocabulary, and comprehension tools for hands-on learning and fun.

School Collaborations & Events

Books by Level

Kindergarten (Ages 3–5)

Early phonics, letter recognition, and playful stories to build foundational skills.

  • The Alphabet Adventure — Fun letter sounds and bright illustrations.
  • Colors & Shapes Magic — Interactive story with simple games.
  • First Words Flashcards — Engaging vocabulary builders.

Lower Primary (Ages 6–8)

Blending, decoding, and early reading fluency with creative stories.

  • Magical Word Builders — Phonics and decoding practice through stories.
  • Reading Sparks — Short chapter books with comprehension questions.
  • Vocabulary Voyage — Fun themed vocabulary flashcards.

Upper Primary (Ages 9–11)

Vocabulary building, comprehension, and creative writing inspiration.

  • Storyweaver’s Journal — Creative writing prompts and exercises.
  • Advanced Reading Sparks — Longer stories to challenge comprehension.
  • Word Wizards — Advanced vocabulary flashcards.
